FOODIELETTE

Looking for a fun place to eat but not sure what you want? Let the roulette decide where to go with your crew! Foodielette (Foodie + Roulette) is a mobile app to help people who eat out with their family and friends decide where to eat while cutting down the time and stress that could be spent on researching many different restaurants.
