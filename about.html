ABOUT YUKI

Hello, thank you for visiting my portfolio website! Originally from Japan, I am a UX/UI designer based in Utah, United States. On top of project management skills, I specialize in website/app designs and Mini-Kawa* illustrations. I enjoy working with teams and solving problems to achieve the best experience for customers as well as to fulfill the business needs. I am a enthusiastic hiker and a nature lover who climbed Mt. Fuji in summer 2022!

*The “Mini-Kawa” style is the combination of minimalism and the Japanese “Kawaii” aesthetic.
